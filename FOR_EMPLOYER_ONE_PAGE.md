# Understanding Your Vault Balances After Month Close

Dear Employer,

## Your Question:
**"After closing the month on June 1st, why don't the vaults start at K0? Where is this money coming from?"**

## The Answer:
**The money you see in the vaults is REAL CASH from May that's still physically sitting there.**

When you "closed the month" on June 1st:
- ✅ You created an accounting record marking the end of May
- ❌ You did NOT remove the physical cash from the vaults

### Think of it like this:
If you have K10,000 in a cash box on May 31st at night, and the calendar changes to June 1st in the morning, **the K10,000 is still in the cash box!** It doesn't disappear just because it's a new month.

## Why Opening Balances Matter

**Imagine this scenario:**

- **May 31:** Vault has K14,590
- **June 1 morning:** Someone steals K5,000
- **June 1-3:** You collect K10,000

**Without tracking opening balance:**
- You'd only see K10,000 collected
- You wouldn't know K5,000 was stolen

**With opening balance:**
- Opening: K14,590
- Collections: K10,000  
- Expected: K24,590
- Actual: K9,590
- **You immediately catch the K15,000 theft!**

This is why all businesses (banks, shops, everyone) track opening balances.
