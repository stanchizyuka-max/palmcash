About Palm Cash

Empowering Zambian communities through innovative financial solutions and transparent loan management.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, transparent, and efficient loan management services that empower individuals and businesses across Zambia to achieve their financial goals.

Our Vision

To become Zambia's leading digital financial platform, fostering economic growth and financial inclusion through innovative technology and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Palm Cash emerged from a vision to revolutionize the lending industry in Zambia through technology and transparency.

Our journey began when our founders recognized the challenges faced by both borrowers and lenders in the traditional loan management process. Long queues, paperwork delays, and lack of transparency were common issues that needed addressing.

With a deep understanding of the Zambian financial landscape and expertise in technology, we developed Palm Cash as a comprehensive digital platform that streamlines the entire loan lifecycle - from application to repayment.

Today, we serve individuals, small businesses, and agricultural enterprises across Zambia, providing them with fair access to credit and the tools they need to manage their financial obligations effectively. Our name "Palm Cash" symbolizes growth, prosperity, and the natural abundance we aim to bring to our customers' financial lives.
