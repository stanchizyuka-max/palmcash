KEY FEATURES
============

1. MINIMUM 15 GROUPS REQUIREMENT
   - Officers must manage ‚â•15 active groups to approve loans
   - Prevents over-extension of credit
   - Ensures portfolio diversification

2. SECURITY DEPOSIT (10% REQUIREMENT)
   - Required before loan disbursement
   - Calculated as 10% of principal
   - Must be verified by manager/admin
   - Tracked separately from payments

3. ROLE-BASED ACCESS CONTROL
   - Admin: Full system access
   - Manager: Operational oversight
   - Officer: Personal workload only
   - Borrower: Own loans only

4. EXPENSE TRACKING
   - Categories: Salaries, Rent, Utilities, Marketing, etc.
   - Approval workflow: Pending ‚Üí Approved/Rejected
   - Separate from loan payments
   - Included in P&L calculations

5. VAULT & BRANCH MANAGEMENT
   - Track cash movements
   - Calculate branch balances
   - Monitor inflows and outflows
   - Support inter-branch transfers

6. COMPREHENSIVE REPORTING
   - Daily transaction reports
   - Profit/loss dashboard
   - Expense reports
   - Collections analysis
   - Security deposit tracking
