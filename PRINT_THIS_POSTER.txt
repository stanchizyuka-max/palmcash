================================================================================
                          ⚠️  IMPORTANT TO KNOW
================================================================================

  ❌ DON'T use Vault Balance for monthly performance
     (It includes cash from previous months)

  ✅ DO use Activity Report for monthly performance
     (Shows only current month)


  📌 Vault Balance = Total cash in vault (May + June + all months)
  📌 Activity Report = Current month only (June only)


  Both are correct. Both are needed. Different purposes.


================================================================================
                              WHY THIS MATTERS
================================================================================

  When you closed the month on June 1st:
  • You created an accounting record ✅
  • You did NOT remove cash from vault ❌

  The money from May is still in the vault.
  That's why vault balance looks "high."

  But Activity Report shows only June's activity.
  That's what you want for tracking performance!


================================================================================
                          DO THIS EVERY MONTH
================================================================================

  ✅ End of month: Run Activity Report for full month
  ✅ Record: Collections, Disbursements, Collection Rate
  ✅ Count: Physical cash in vault (should match Vault Balance)
  ✅ Close: Click "Month Close" button
  ✅ Next month: Run Activity Report again with new dates

                         EXAMPLE: JUNE 2026
================================================================================

  Activity Report (Jun 1-3):
  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  Collections:        K11,791   ← What June brought in             │
  │  Disbursements:      K14,000   ← What June paid out               │

  │  Collection Rate:    94%                                           │

  Vault Balance (KAMWALA SOUTH):
  ┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  Daily:              K200      ← Total cash in daily vault        │

  │  TOTAL:              K30,000   ← All cash (May + June)            │
  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Both numbers are correct!
  One shows monthly performance (K11,791 collected in June)
  Other shows total cash on hand (K30,000 total)
