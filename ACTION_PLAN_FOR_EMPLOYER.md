# ACTION PLAN: How to Track "What Each Month Brings In"

## Your Question:
**"How do we track what each month brings into our vaults?"**

---

## What You Need to Do (3 Simple Steps):

### STEP 1: Go to Activity Report
1. Log into Palm Cash system
2. Click **"Dashboard"** in the top menu
3. Click **"Activity Report"** (or "System Activity Report")

### STEP 2: Set the Date Range
1. In the **"From"** date field, enter: **June 1, 2026**
2. In the **"To"** date field, enter: **June 30, 2026** (or today's date)
3. Select the branch you want to view (or "All Branches")
4. Click **"Filter"** or **"Search"**

### STEP 3: Read the Numbers
The report will show you:

```
Collections:        K11,791  ← Money collected from borrowers in June
Disbursements:      K14,000  ← Money given out as loans in June
Activities:         147      ← Number of transactions in June
Clients:            88       ← Number of clients who transacted in June
Collection Rate:    94%      ← Percentage of expected payments collected
```

**The "Collections" number (K11,791) is what June brought into your vaults.**

---

## That's It! You're Done.

**This is how you track monthly performance.**

Every month:
1. Go to Activity Report
2. Set dates for that month
3. Look at Collections number

---

## What About the Vault Balance Pages?

**The Vault Balance pages show something DIFFERENT.**

### Vault Balance = Total Cash in Vault (All Time)

The vault pages show:
- Cash from May (that was already there)
- PLUS cash from June (new collections)
- = Total cash physically in the vault today

**Example: KAMWALA SOUTH Weekly Vault shows K29,800**

This means:
```
K14,590   Money from May (was in vault on May 31)
+K15,210  Money collected in June
────────
K29,800   Total cash in vault right now
```

### Why Both Numbers Exist:

| Page | What It Shows | Use It For |
|------|---------------|------------|
| **Activity Report** | Only current month's activity | Track "what this month brought in" |
| **Vault Balance** | Total cash on hand (all months) | Security, reconciliation, "how much cash do we have" |

---

## Quick Summary:

✅ **To track "what June brings in":** Use Activity Report (Jun 1-30)  
✅ **To see total cash in vault:** Use Vault Balance pages  

**Both are correct. Both are needed. They just show different things.**

---

## Why the Vault Doesn't Start at K0 After Month Close:

**Simple answer:** The money from May is still physically in the vault.

When you "close the month":
- ✅ You create a record for accounting
- ❌ You DON'T remove physical cash from the vault

Think of it like your wallet:
- If you have K500 in your wallet on May 31 night
- You still have K500 in your wallet on June 1 morning
- It doesn't disappear because the calendar changed

**Same with vaults:** The K14,590 that was in KAMWALA vault on May 31 is still there on June 1. It's real cash.

---

## What You Should Do Each Month:

### End of Month Routine:

1. **Run Activity Report** for the full month
   - Example: Jun 1 - Jun 30
   
2. **Record these numbers in your reports:**
   - Collections: K[amount]
   - Disbursements: K[amount]
   - Net: K[amount]
   - Collection Rate: XX%

3. **Count physical cash in vaults**
   - Should match Vault Balance page
   - If it doesn't match, investigate immediately

4. **Close the month** (click Month Close button)
   - This creates an accounting record
   - Cash stays in vault, carries forward to next month

5. **Next month starts:**
   - Vault has: Previous month balance + new activity
   - Activity Report shows: Only new month's transactions

## Common Mistakes to Avoid:

❌ **Don't use Vault Balance to track monthly performance**
- Vault Balance includes all previous months
- Not useful for "what this month brought in"

❌ **Don't expect vaults to reset to K0 after month close**
- Cash from previous month carries forward
- This is standard accounting
- All businesses do this

✅ **Do use Activity Report for monthly tracking**
- Shows only current month
- Perfect for performance measurement

✅ **Do use Vault Balance for security**
- Shows total cash you should have
- Compare with physical count
- Catch theft or discrepancies

## If You Still Have Questions:

### Question: "Where did the K14,590 in KAMWALA vault come from?"
**Answer:** It was there on May 31 when you closed May. It's real cash from May's operations. It didn't disappear overnight.

### Question: "Can we make it start at K0 each month?"
**Answer:** Only if you physically remove all cash from the vault (deposit to bank, move to another location, etc.). Otherwise, it stays in the vault and must be tracked.

### Question: "How do I compare June to May?"
**Answer:** 
1. Run Activity Report for May 1-31 → Note Collections
2. Run Activity Report for June 1-30 → Note Collections
3. Compare the two numbers

### Question: "Why didn't the system tell us this before?"
**Answer:** This is how all accounting systems work. The system is correct. It's about understanding what each report shows.
