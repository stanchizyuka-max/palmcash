# QUICK REFERENCE: Understanding Vault Balances vs Monthly Performance

---

## ❓ THE QUESTION

**"After we closed the month, why don't the vaults start at K0? Where is this money coming from?"**

---

## ✅ THE ANSWER

**The money you see is REAL CASH from May that's still physically in the vault.**

"Closing the month" = Creating an accounting record  
"Closing the month" ≠ Removing physical cash

---

## 📊 TWO DIFFERENT THINGS YOU'RE TRACKING

### 1️⃣ VAULT BALANCE (Total Cash on Hand)
- **What it shows:** All cash physically in the vault
- **Includes:** Previous months + current month
- **Where to find:** Vault pages
- **Purpose:** Security, reconciliation, "How much cash do we have?"

**Example:** KAMWALA Weekly shows K29,800
- K14,590 from May (was there on May 31)
- K15,210 collected in June
- = K29,800 total

### 2️⃣ MONTHLY PERFORMANCE (What This Month Brought In)
- **What it shows:** Only current month's activity
- **Includes:** ONLY current month transactions
- **Where to find:** Activity Report
- **Purpose:** Performance tracking, "How much did we make this month?"

**Example:** Activity Report (Jun 1-3) shows:
- Collections: K11,791 ← **This is what June brought in**
- Disbursements: K14,000
- Net: -K2,209

---

## 🎯 TO TRACK "WHAT EACH MONTH BRINGS IN"

### USE THE ACTIVITY REPORT ✅

1. Go to **Dashboard → Activity Report**
2. Set date range: **Jun 1 - Jun 30**
3. Select branch (or "All Branches")

**This shows:**
- ✅ Collections (money IN)
- ✅ Disbursements (money OUT)
- ✅ Number of clients
- ✅ Collection rate
- ✅ ONLY current month (no opening balance)

**This is the number you want!**

---

## 💡 SIMPLE ANALOGY

Think of your personal wallet:

**May 31st night:**
- You have K500 in your wallet
- Month ends

**June 1st morning:**
- You still have K500 in your wallet
- **It didn't disappear because the calendar changed!**

**June 1st-3rd:**
- You earn K300 (salary)
- You spend K100 (expenses)

**June 3rd:**
- Wallet balance: K500 + K300 - K100 = **K700**
- June earnings: **K300** (not K700!)

**Vault works the same way:**
- Vault balance = Total cash (includes May's money)
- June earnings = Activity Report (only June)

## ⚠️ WHY WE NEED BOTH NUMBERS

### Scenario: Catching Theft

**Without opening balance:**
- June collections: K10,000
- Vault has: K9,000
- You think: "We collected K10,000, have K9,000... close enough?"

**With opening balance:**
- May 31: K14,590 in vault
- June collections: K10,000
- Expected: K24,590
- Actual: K9,000
- **MISSING: K15,590 - THEFT DETECTED!**

This is why banks, shops, and ALL businesses track opening balances.
