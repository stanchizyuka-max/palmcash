## THE REALITY

### What "Month Closing" Actually Does:

✅ Creates an accounting record marking end of May  
✅ Allows you to generate May performance reports  
✅ Sets a timestamp for "May is finished"  

❌ Does NOT remove physical cash from vaults  
❌ Does NOT reset vault balances to K0  
❌ Does NOT make the money disappear  

### Think of it Like This:

**May 31st, 10pm:**
- You count the cash in KAMWALA vault
- You find K14,590 in there
- You write in your book: "May 31: Closing balance K14,590"

**June 1st, 8am:**
- You open the vault
- The same K14,590 is still there
- It's now called "Opening balance for June"
- **The physical cash didn't vanish overnight!**

## WHY OPENING BALANCES MATTER (Explain to Employer)

### Security & Accountability:

Imagine this scenario:

**May 31st:** Vault has K14,590  
**June 1st morning:** Someone steals K5,000  
**June 1-3:** You collect K10,000 from borrowers  

**If you don't track opening balance:**
- You'd only see K10,000 collected
- Vault would have K9,590 (14,590 - 5,000 + 10,000)
- **You'd never know K5,000 was stolen!**

**With opening balance tracked:**
- Opening: K14,590
- Collections: K10,000
- Expected: K24,590
- Actual: K9,590
- **Missing: K15,000 - You catch the theft immediately!**

This is why ALL businesses (banks, shops, everyone) track opening balances.
