================================================================================
           UNDERSTANDING VAULT BALANCES AFTER MONTH CLOSING
================================================================================

THE QUESTION:
-------------
"After closing the month, why don't vaults start at K0?"
"Where is this money coming from?"

THE ANSWER:
-----------
The money is REAL CASH from May that's still in the vault!

Month closing = Creating a record (accounting)
Month closing ≠ Removing physical cash

Vault works the SAME way:
  - Vault balance = Total cash (includes May)
  - June earnings = Activity Report (only June)


================================================================================
                        WHY BOTH NUMBERS ARE NEEDED
================================================================================

SCENARIO: Catching Theft
────────────────────────

WITHOUT opening balance tracking:
  ✗ June collections: K10,000
  ✗ Vault has: K9,000
  ✗ You think: "Close enough..."
  ✗ You MISS the theft!

WITH opening balance tracking:
  ✓ May 31 closing: K14,590
  ✓ June collections: K10,000
  ✓ Expected balance: K24,590
  ✓ Actual balance: K9,000
  ✓ MISSING: K15,590 ← THEFT DETECTED! 🚨

This is why ALL businesses track opening balances:
  • Banks
  • Shops
  • Supermarkets
  • Everyone!
